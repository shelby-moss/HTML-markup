What About Lions?

Here are the Basics

Lions are so big. They live in Africa and stick together in "prides." They hunt basically anything they can and have a great time doing it.

Different Types of Lions

  • Male Lions
    • Roles of Male Lions
    Sit around and be lazy until he needs to protect something
  • Female Lions
    • Roles of Female Lions
    Have the babies, hunt the food, keep everyone alive
  • Baby Lions
    • Roles of Baby Lions
    Be a nuisance and learn how to survive

Why Are People Afraid of Lions?

Basically, people are afraid of lions because they can and will kill you. But, as stated in the song "The Lion Sleeps Tonight," you can always sleep peacefully as long as you know that the lions are also asleep and not trying to kill you.
